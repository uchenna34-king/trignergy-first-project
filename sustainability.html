Health, Safety and Environment
Statement,Trig Energy limited is
committed to:
A policy that enables all work activities to be carried out safely, with all feasible measures taken to eliminate (or at least reduce) risks to the health, safety, and welfare of workers and anyone else whose health, safety, or welfare may be affected by our operations.
Assuring maximum compliance with applicable Health and Safety legislation, Codes of Practice, and Standards.
Ensure that all employees have the necessary skills and training to perform their duties; Provide and maintain a safe work environment and procedures to prevent accidents and cases of work-related illness; Maintain safe and healthy working conditions, and review and revise this policy as needed and on a regular basis
ENVIRONMENT, HEALTH, SAFETY, AND QUALITY OBJECTIVES
Our Vision
  • • Trig Energy aims to store and deliver products and services which would guarantee customers satisfaction. To accomplish this we plan to.
  • • Ensure on-time quality service and product delivery to her customers.
  • • To engage our customers to understand their requirements and render our services to meet and exceed their expectations.
  • • To ensure as an organization the stated code of conducts and regulations requirements are adhered to strictly.
Our Mission
  • • To be proactive in putting in place preventive and recovery barriers in place for risk of fire and product spillage in our facility and the distribution network.
  • • To ensure that this objective and retained and implemented at every level in the organization.
  • • To ensure the products and services rendered are carried out in a safe manner that ensures minimal impact on the environment and to ensure the safety of all stakeholders.
  • • The head of EHS would review and monitor the implementation of this policy from time to time and may amend the same as required to ensure compliance and effectiveness.
